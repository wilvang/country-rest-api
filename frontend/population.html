Population Information

To retrieve population information about a country, please add the ISO 3166-2 identifier of the country to the URL path.

Example: /countryinfo/v1/population/{ISO3166-2}

You can also use the ?limit={startYear-endYear} query parameter to specify the range of years (boundary values are included).

Example: /countryinfo/v1/population/{ISO3166-2}?limit=2000-2020

This will display the population for the country for the specified years and calculate the mean of all the years displayed.

Available paths:

  • Home
  • Info
  • Status
